Team Introduction

The team has achieved a good foundation in deep learning, intelligent computing, and visualization, and the results have been published in journals and conferences such as CVPR, Journal of Computational and Applied Mathematics, Computers&Mathematics with Applications, Computer Aided Design, Computer Graphics Forum, Graphical Models, Computers&Graphics, Visual Computer, and Software Journal.

The related work on protein sequence structure prediction and functional analysis has been published in journals such as Briefings in Bioinformatics, Bioinformatics, Plos Computational Biology, BMC Genomics, BMC Bioinformatics, EEE/ACM Transactions on Computational Biology and Bioinformatics.
