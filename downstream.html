Epidemic Strain Analysis

Semantic syntax analysis of epidemic strains. This function calculates the relevant locations of Vocs (variants of concerns) through semantic and syntactic changes, and thus analyzes the locations that are likely to undergo immune escape. Here we provide two examples of Vocs analyzes. If you have your own data that you would like to analyze, please contact us according to the contact information on the "Contact Us" page.
